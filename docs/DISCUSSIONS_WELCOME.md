# Welcome to crisis-mesh-messenger Discussions! 🛰️💬🗺️

We are thrilled to welcome you to the community! This repository is built to provide completely off-grid communication software for disaster areas, search-and-rescue teams, and remote communities worldwide.

---

## 🧭 What is Crisis Mesh Messenger?
When the grid goes down due to natural disasters, conflict, or infrastructure failure, this app turns standard consumer hardware (smartphones, local routers, off-the-shelf sub-GHz radio modules) into a decentralized store-and-forward communication network. 

---

## 🛠️ Key Topics to Discuss & Ideas to Propose

We want your help, ideas, and suggestions to make this platform bulletproof. Here are some of our primary focus areas:
1. **Low-Energy & Battery routing protocols**: How can we optimize node-forwarding decisions to consume even less power on handheld devices?
2. **Sub-GHz LoRa integration**: Proposals for off-the-shelf hardware attachments (e.g. Heltec V3, T-Beam, Meshtastic links) to extend off-grid ranges up to miles.
3. **Advanced Rescue Vitals**: Scoring formulas for helicopter landing zones, local supply tracking thresholds, and off-grid emergency QR profiles.
4. **AR Viewfinder enhancements**: Evacuation compass overlays, off-grid navigation grids, and offline map cached tile overlays.
